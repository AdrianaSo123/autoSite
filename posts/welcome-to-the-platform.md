# Welcome to the AI Publishing Platform

This platform is designed to make publishing effortless. Record your thoughts, and our AI pipeline will transform them into polished blog posts.

## How It Works

1. **Record** — Capture your ideas using the recording app
2. **Process** — AI transcribes and structures your content
3. **Publish** — Posts appear on your blog automatically
