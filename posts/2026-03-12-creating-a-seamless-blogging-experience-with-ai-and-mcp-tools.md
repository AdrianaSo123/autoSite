I recently embarked on an exciting project where I developed a recording app using AI. This app is designed to enhance the way I create and manage content. In addition to the app, I built a website featuring a chat interface. The combination of these two technologies allows me to effortlessly communicate with an app and auto-publish my blog posts, as you can see here.

To streamline the process even further, I've integrated multiple MCP (Multi-Channel Publishing) tools. These tools significantly expedite the blogging workflow, making it more efficient and less time-consuming.

This project not only showcases the power of AI in content creation but also highlights the potential of automation in blogging. By leveraging these technologies, I can focus more on creating quality content while the system handles the repetitive tasks.
