AI is different. It can look across huge amounts of information and find patterns, relationships, and insights that might take humans much longer to see. Because of this, AI isn't just a tool for automation; it's becoming a system for connecting ideas.

AI also changes how we interact with technology. Instead of learning complicated commands or programming languages, we can simply talk to AI. That makes technology feel less like a machine and more like a collaborator.

In many ways, AI is shifting computing from something technical to something conversational. And that might be one of the biggest changes of all.
